Collection of information:
You can browse through our website without sharing your personal information with us. In this case, we only record access data such as name of the internet service provider, the website you are visiting us from and the browser information. Personal data is only being recorded if you voluntarily register for it when creating a new customer account or when registering for a newsletter.
Information collected by Dinesh Hosiery: 
(a) Personal Information - Name, gender, marital status and date of birth.
(b) Contact Details - Postal address, email address and phone number
(c) Payment details - Your payment details such as credit card number, cardholder name, expiration date and CVV or other information as required for internet banking or other payment instruments is not held by Dinesh Hosiery but is held by our Payment Gateway partner (PayU). Our payment gateway partner is "VeriSign Secured" and "PCI-Compliant" which ensure the highest standards of safety and security for your information.

(e) Cookies: Cookie is a piece of data stored by a website within the web browser while you are browsing that website. It enables the website to remember information such as items in the shopping cart or to record your browsing history in order to show relevant content in the future. However, most modern browsers allow the users to manage the settings of the cookies i.e. enable, disable or delete particular cookies.
(f) On-site information such as pages visited, traffic source, IP address, browser information, date and time of browsing.
(g) Third party sign in information shared on our website such as Facebook, Twitter, Pinterest and YouTube.
(h) Information through communication: All information shared through communication on email, messages or phone such as queries related to orders, products or complaints.
(i) Google Analytics advertising features: Various cookies will be used to identify the user base. A user can opt out of this feature through Ads Settings, Ad Settings for mobile apps, or through Google Analytics' currently available opt-outs  for the web.
